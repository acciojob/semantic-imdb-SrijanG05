Plot Summary

Pulp Fiction is a non-linear crime masterpiece that weaves together the lives of two hitmen, a boxer, and a gangster's wife. Their stories intersect in a series of funny, bizarre, and violent incidents involving a mysterious briefcase, a diner robbery, and a high-stakes date that goes dangerously wrong.
